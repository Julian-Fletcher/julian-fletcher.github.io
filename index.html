Constrained Pathfinding Algorithms

In Progress
Algorithm Design & Optimization

This research focuses on the development, implementation, and optimization of two related methods for computing shortest paths subject to turn constraints. The work involves improving an existing modification to Dijkstra's algorithm to efficiently handle turn angle and length requirements when finding paths.

The project aims to demonstrate comparable performance to previous methods despite solving a more complex problem.

C++ CMake Python Algorithm Design Shortest Paths

Flood Extent Mapping in MN

Fall 2024
Project & Poster Presentation

Utilized Sentinel-1 SAR data to detect and map flooding near Mankato, Minnesota along the Minnesota River. The workflow included the Sentinel Application Platform (SNAP) and ArcGIS Pro to process and display SAR data from 2023 and 2024. By classifying water pixels and layering imagery, I effectively mapped the extent of the flooding.
